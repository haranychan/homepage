Cartesian Genetic Programmingを用いた転用可能な積み付けアルゴリズムの自動生成
This paper proposes an automatic construction technique of transferable loading algorithms based on Cartesian Genetic Programming. The proposed method aims to construct the loading algorithm with a few hundred fitness evaluations by optimizing the execution order of rules to decide a type of multiple cardboard boxes and their loadable positions simultaneously.
